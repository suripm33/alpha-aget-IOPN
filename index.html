Season 1: DeFi & Open Finance on OPN Chain

Alpha Agent

Alpha Agent is an AI assistant for DeFi builders and users to research OPN Chain protocols, prepare swap, locker, and perp workflows, and understand on-chain finance tasks.

What it does

Alpha Agent helps users explore DeFi opportunities on OPN Chain by turning complex financial workflows into clear step-by-step guidance. It is designed to support protocol research, task preparation, and safer decision-making for DeFi users.

DeFi Use Cases

  • Research OPN Chain DeFi protocols
  • Prepare swap workflow checklists
  • Understand locker and liquidity actions
  • Plan perp and open finance task flows
  • Review contract and transaction context before acting

Why it matters

DeFi can be confusing for new users because they need to understand wallets, networks, contracts, liquidity, swaps, approvals, and risks. Alpha Agent aims to make these workflows easier to understand before users interact on-chain.

On-chain Proof

Alpha Agent includes an AlphaAgentProof smart contract deployed on OPN Chain testnet as proof of the builder submission.
